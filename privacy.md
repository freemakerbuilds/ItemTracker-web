# Privacy Policy for ItemTracker #

ItemTracker is designed to store all data locally on your device. 
### Third-Party Services
The application does not integrate or utilize any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks (such as Firebase, Google Analytics, or AdMob) that collect, track, or transmit user data.

- We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or usage information to external servers.
- All information you log in the application remains entirely on your local device.
### Data Retention and Deletion
Since all data is stored exclusively within your device's local storage, we do not retain, store, or manage your information on external servers. You can permanently delete all logged data at any time by clearing the application data via Android system settings or by uninstalling the application from your device.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected directly on this web page with an updated revision date.
